SunNet is a towny server where you create factions with others to rule the map. In this server you can build your empires strength through working together as a group to farm, or going to war with other factions to take loot to strengthen your army and take over the map. With our new terralith that will change how the terrain looks from the normal flat blocky world to a newly shaped world with spectacular scenery. Shaders highly recommended.
